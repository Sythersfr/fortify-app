Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my streak?

Go to the Progress tab, scroll down, and tap the "Reset" button. Remember, His mercies are new every morning.

How does the AI Faith Guide work?

Our AI Faith Guide provides Biblical wisdom and encouragement 24/7. It's trained to respond with Scripture-based guidance and compassion.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your personal journey data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account". This action is permanent.

Can I use Fortify offline?

Basic features like viewing your streak work offline. The AI Faith Guide and Community features require an internet connection.
